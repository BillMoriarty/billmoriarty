Errors & How to Solve Them

[ufw runUIApplicationMainWithArgc: argc argv: argv];

For anyone else that runs into this error the solution was two-fold.

  • You have to uncheck "Strip Engine Code" in Unity under player settings > IOS.
  • Then in Xcode, you have to find (under either Swift or Apple Clang - code generation) a setting called 'Optimization Level' and set it to none. https://forum.unity.com/threads/xcode-error-when-building-thread-1-exc_bad_access.802374/
